Welcome to the Dicey Luck!









How to play this super awesome game:



  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.

  • In each turn, a player rolls 2 dice as many      times as he whishes. Each result get added      to his ROUND score.

  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1 on either of the      dice, all his ROUND score gets lost. After      that, it's the next player's turn.

  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which      means that his ROUND score gets added      to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the      next player's turn.

  • The first player to reach 100 points on      GLOBAL score wins the game, you can      also change the final winning score.
